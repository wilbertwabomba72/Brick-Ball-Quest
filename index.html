A satisfying brick-breaking challenge

Brick Ball Quest is a clean portrait game about finding the perfect angle. Drag to aim, release to launch your balls, and use rebounds to clear numbered bricks before they reach the danger line.

Collect bonus balls, trigger bomb items, earn stars, and progress through 50 levels with steadily rising difficulty.

Features

  • Simple aimingDrag and release to send balls bouncing across the board.
  • 50 levelsProgressive layouts keep every stage quick and readable.
  • Bomb itemsTrigger explosive clears when the board gets crowded.
  • Bonus ballsGrow your shot count and create bigger chain reactions.
  • Star ratingsReplay levels and improve your best move count.
  • Offline playEnjoy the core game anywhere, with local progress saving.
