Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Brick Ball Quest handles information when you use the game. If you have questions, contact the developer at 508923216@qq.com.

Information We Collect

Brick Ball Quest does not require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, or other direct contact information inside the game.

The game stores basic gameplay progress locally on your device, such as unlocked levels, star ratings, best moves, and music or sound settings. This information is used only to provide the game experience and is not sent to the developer.

Advertising and Third-Party Services

Brick Ball Quest may include third-party advertising SDKs to show ads. These third-party services may collect or process limited device and advertising-related information, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, ad interactions, approximate location derived from network information, crash information, and performance data.

Where required by iOS, the game uses Apple’s App Tracking Transparency permission before allowing tracking for advertising purposes. You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings at any time.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Save local game progress and settings.
  • Provide and improve gameplay features.
  • Display and measure ads through third-party advertising services.
  • Maintain app stability and diagnose technical issues.

Data Sharing

The developer does not sell personal information. Third-party advertising providers may process data according to their own privacy policies and applicable law. The developer does not control all data practices of these third-party services.

Children’s Privacy

Brick Ball Quest is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact the developer and reasonable steps will be taken to address the issue.

Data Retention

Local gameplay progress remains on your device until you delete the app or reset your device data. Data processed by third-party advertising services is retained according to those services’ own policies.

Your Choices

You can disable personalized tracking through iOS App Tracking Transparency controls, limit ad tracking in system settings where available, or delete the app to remove locally stored game progress from your device.

Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to support app security. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: 508923216@qq.com.
